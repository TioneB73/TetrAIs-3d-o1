    🎮 Game Controls

    Learn how to master TetrAIs 3D with keyboard, mouse and buttons controls!

    🖥️ Keyboard Controls:

    • Arrow Keys: Move pieces left, right, down.
    • Spacebar: Drop piece instantly.
    • J, K ,L : Rotate X, Y, Z Tetrominos.

    🖱️ Mouse Controls and 👆 Phone & Tablet Tap Screen

    • 🖱️ Left-Click🖱️ or 👆 Tap & Drag: Move piece.
    • 🖱️ Right-Click or Button 👆: Rotate Tetrominos.
    • 🖱️ Wheel-Click or Wheel 👆: Rotate or Zoom Grid.

    📚 How to Play & Game Rules

    🔹 Scoring System:

    • 1 Line cleared: 100 pts
    • 2 Lines cleared: 300 pts
    • 3 Lines cleared: 500 pts
    • 4 Lines cleared: 800 pts
    • More lines cleared: more points!

    🔸 Shape Unlocking:

    Unlock new shapes at scores: 600, 1200, 2000 pts and more!

    🏅 Bonus Rewards:

    Bonus multipliers activate randomly when consecutive lines are cleared, significantly boosting your score!

    📊 Understanding Status Bars:

    • Pieces Bar: Counts from 25 pieces per level.
    • Progress Bar: Current grid completion percentage.
    • Speed Bar: Starts at 600, adjusts by gameplay.
    • High Score Bar: Current vs. highest score.
